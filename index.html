About Panadura Florists

Trusted Funeral Directors with over
30 Years of History

Panadura Florists is a well-respected independent family-owned funeral service provider. We are reputed for providing professional service, personal support, and guidance for our clients during times of distress. We offer a comprehensive range of funeral packages—traditional or bespoke—tailored to suit the diverse needs of every faith and religion in Sri Lanka.
