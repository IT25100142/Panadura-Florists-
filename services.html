Our Services

Funeral Management

We take the weight off your shoulders by handling every aspect of the funeral arrangement. From the moment you contact us, our experienced funeral directors will guide you through legal documentation, cemetery or crematorium booking, and ceremony planning.

  • Legal Registration Support
  • Newspaper Notice Placements
  • Venue Coordination
  • Religious Clergy Arrangement

Professional Embalming

Our qualified embalmers use state-of-the-art techniques to preserve the dignity of your loved one. We also offer mobile freezer facilities for home funerals, ensuring preservation and hygiene are maintained throughout the wake.

  • Hygienic Treatment
  • Dressing & Cosmetology
  • Mobile Freezer Units

Luxury Fleet

We possess one of the finest fleets of ceremonial vehicles in Sri Lanka. Choose from our signature Black & Gold hearses, modern Mercedes Benz units, or classic Volvo hearses. We also provide flower vans and family transport coaches.

Traditional Ransivige

For traditional Buddhist funerals, we construct elaborate and respectful Ransivige (Funeral Pyre Structures). Our skilled craftsmen create distinct designs that honor the cultural heritage and status of the deceased.

  • Custom Pyre Design
  • Traditional Decorations
  • Gold & White Themes
